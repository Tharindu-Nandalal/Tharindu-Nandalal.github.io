PhD Researcher in Computational Mechanics

Institute for Engineering and Computational Mechanics (ITM), University of Stuttgart

I am a PhD student working on computational and experimental modelling of classical guitars. My research focuses on finite element modelling of guitar soundboards, structural dynamics, and the influence of wood material variability on acoustic performance.
